I am a first year Computer Science PhD student in Interactive School of Computing at Georgia Institue of Technology. I am advised by Prof. Munmun De Choudhury. I am currently a part of Social Dynamics and Well Being lab and am working on "Online support and Mental Health". I am interested in studying how different types of support like informational, emotional etc. impact an individual’s perceived sense of mental well-being in an online community, and how these support types relate to an online community’s helpfulness, efficacy, and survival over time.
