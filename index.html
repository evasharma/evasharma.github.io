Eva Sharma

I am a second year Computer Science PhD student in School of Interactive Computing at Georgia Institue of Technology, advised by Prof. Munmun De Choudhury. Prior to Georgia Tech, I completed my Master's degree in Computer Science from Cornell University and my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Engineering from SRM University.

I am currently part of Social Dynamics and Well Being Lab and my research interests are in the area of computational social sciences. I aim to leverage computational methods and resources in order to understand and reason about complex social processes underlying human behavior in large-scale online social systems.
